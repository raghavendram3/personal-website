Work Summary

As a doctoral researcher at Wageningen University & Research, I regularly perform first-principle simulations for chemical reactions. I employ computational approaches such as density functional theory (DFT), ab initio molecular dynamics (AIMD), and microkinetic modelling (MKM) to investigate catalytic mechanisms and kinetics. I aim to design affordable and efficient catalysts for converting biomass to platform chemicals for biofuels, contributing to a sustainable and circular economy.
