Peer-Reviewed Publications

Ground-state properties of the narrowest zigzag graphene nanoribbon from quantum Monte Carlo and comparison with density functional theory (2022)

Journal: Journal of Chemical Physics

Abstract: This study used quantum Monte Carlo calculations to examine the ground-state properties of the narrowest zigzag graphene nanoribbon. The research revealed significant antiferromagnetic stabilization energy and magnetization, suggesting that antiferromagnetic correlations could persist above room temperature.

Revealing Active Sites and Reaction Pathways in Methane Non‐Oxidative Coupling over Iron‐Containing Zeolites (2023)

Journal: Angewandte Chemie

Abstract: This work explored methane non-oxidative coupling using siliceous [Fe]zeolites with MFI and CHA topologies. The study employed advanced spectroscopic techniques to elucidate active sites and intermediates, proposing a reaction network leading to polyaromatic species.

Surface-Activated Mechano-Catalysis for Ambient Conversion of Plastic Waste (2024)

Journal: Journal of the American Chemical Society

Abstract: This research demonstrated a novel mechano-catalytic strategy for plastic waste conversion at ambient conditions. The study showed that surface activation of zirconia grinding spheres creates redox active sites, enabling the formation of hydrocarbons from polypropylene without additional heating.

Toward the Rational Design of More Efficient Mo2C Catalysts for Hydrodeoxygenation–Mechanism and Descriptor Identification (2023)

Journal: ACS Catalysis

Abstract: This work investigated the reaction mechanism of butyric acid hydrodeoxygenation over molybdenum carbide (Mo2C). The study identified rate-determining steps and explored heteroatom doping to enhance catalytic activity, providing strategies for rational catalyst design.

Comparison of RNC Coupling and CO Coupling Mediated by Cr–Cr Quintuple Bond and B–B Multiple Bonds: Main Group Metallomimetics (2021)

Journal: Journal of Physical Chemistry A

Abstract: This research delved into the theoretical and computational chemistry aspects of Cr–Cr quintuple bonds and B–B multiple bonds, providing insights into main group metallomimetics.
