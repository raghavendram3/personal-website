Research Experience

Doctoral Student Oct 2020 - Present
Wageningen University, NL
Supervisors: Dr. Guanna Li, Prof. Han Zuilhof, Prof. Harry Bitter
  • Developed a multiscale modelling methodology to rationalize and optimize the catalytic activity of transition metal carbides for biomass conversion using density functional theory (DFT), microkinetic modelling (MKM), and ab initio molecular dynamics (AIMD).
  • Used machine learning approach (SISSO) to identify interpretable descriptors of activity for transition metal carbides.
  • Trained ML potentials (MACE, fair-chem) on AIMD-and-metadynamics trajectories to accelerate AIMD simulations for transition metal carbides.
  • Provided insights into the active site in surface-activated mechanocatalysis over zirconia for ambient conversion of plastic waste.
  • Provided mechanistic insights into non-oxidative methane coupling inside Fe[MFI] zeolite pore.
Master's Thesis May 2019 - May 2020
Sorbonne Université, FR
