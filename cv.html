Research Experience

Doctoral Student Oct 2020 - Present
Wageningen University, NL
Supervisors: Dr. Guanna Li, Prof. Han Zuilhof, Prof. Harry Bitter
  • Developed a multiscale modelling methodology to rationalize and optimize the catalytic activity of transition metal carbides for biomass conversion using DFT, MKM, and AIMD.
  • Used a machine learning approach (SISSO) to identify interpretable descriptors of activity for transition metal carbides.
  • Trained ML potentials (MACE, fair-chem) on AIMD-and-metadynamics trajectories to accelerate AIMD simulations.
  • Provided insights into the active site in surface-activated mechanocatalysis over zirconia for plastic waste conversion.
  • Investigated non-oxidative methane coupling inside Fe[MFI] zeolite pore.
Master's Thesis May 2019 - May 2020
Sorbonne Université, FR
Supervisors: Prof. Michele Casula, Prof. Prasenjit Ghosh
  • Developed a methodology using DFT+U and QMC to study magnetic properties of carbon-based materials.
  • Demonstrated that the zigzag graphene nanoribbon (zGNRs) is a correlated quasi-one-dimensional system with localized π electrons exhibiting AFM ordering.
Research Intern Aug 2018 - May 2019
IISER Pune, IN
Supervisor: Prof. Prasenjit Ghosh
  • Evaluated the bandgaps and magnetic properties of zGNRs as a function of width using various DFT approaches.
Research Intern May 2018 - Jul 2018
Indian Institute of Science, IN
