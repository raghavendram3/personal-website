Research Experience

Doctoral Student at Wageningen University, working on the catalytic activity of transition metal carbides for biomass conversion.

  • Developed a multiscale modeling methodology to optimize catalytic activity using DFT, MKM, and AIMD.
  • Applied machine learning (SISSO) to identify interpretable descriptors for transition metal carbide activity.
  • Trained ML potentials (MACE, fair-chem) to accelerate AIMD simulations for transition metal carbides.
  • Investigated active sites in surface-activated mechanocatalysis for plastic waste conversion.
  • Analyzed the mechanism of non-oxidative methane coupling inside Fe[MFI] zeolite pores.
